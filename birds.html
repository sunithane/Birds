About Birds

Birds are fascinating creatures! They belong to the class Aves and are characterized by feathers, beaks without teeth, and the ability to lay hard-shelled eggs. Most birds are adapted for flight, though some, like ostriches and penguins, have evolved to be flightless. There are over 10,000 known species of birds, ranging from tiny hummingbirds to massive eagles and albatrosses. They can be found in nearly every habitat on Earth, from dense forests to open oceans. Many birds are known for their intelligence, problem-solving skills, and even the ability to mimic human speech (like parrots and crows).
